Directions
Please respond to the questions for the Personal Statement and the Personal Essay in an electronic format document and attach it using the file attachment tool below. These questions are to be completed by the applicant. Please save BOTH the Personal Statement and the Essay in one document with the applicant's name as the title of the document.
Applicant's Personal Statement
1. Why did you decide to apply to Rockhurst High School?
2. List three things about yourself that you consider to be strong points.
3. List the activities in which you have participated during the last two years in your school, church and/or community.
4. In addition to academic courses, list the activities you would like to join at Rockhurst High School. If band, name instrument(s).

Applicant's Personal Essay
1. If you could trade places with any living person, who would that person be? Explain your supporting reasons for your selection and what you hope to accomplish in your new role.

Parents' Letter of Recommendation
1. Write a letter of recommendation to the Admissions Committee for your child. This recommendation might include an assessment of your child's strengths and weakness or a statement of why you believe Rockhurst and your son would be a good match.
2. List any relatives that have attended Rockhurst High School. Include the year they graduated and to help keep our information current, please include their current address.
