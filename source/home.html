Weekly News

The Rockhurst Math Team finished in 6th place at the Missouri State Math Competition.  Alex Stubblefield took 4th in the Target Round and 5th in the Sprint Event.  The team of Jack Livers, Andrew Krieger, Yifan Huang, Joey Crowe, Jack Franken and Alex Stubblefield finished 5th in the Power Question.

Rockhurst Model UN had 31 students who participated in the Metro Kansas City Model UN conference April 9 at Johnson County Community College. At what turned out to be one of the most competitive local conferences in recent memory, the Rockhurst Model UN team performed very well. The delegation from Togo, consisting of Juniors Zach Klamann, Hughes Ransom, Joseph Knopke, and Andrew Mohajir won one of only four Outstanding Delegation awards, out of the more than 50 country delegations represented at the conference.
Juniors Kyle Savage, Jack Barry, Kishan Desai, and Noah Ries were recognized for their outstanding position papers.
Honorable mention awards went to Juniors Andrew Mohajir and Michael Healy, as well as sophomore Calaway Scholes. Junior Joseph Knopke won a distinguished delegate award. Juniors Zach Klamann and Hughes Ransom won recognition as the most outstanding delegates on their committees.
Klamann and Ransom completed the year with awards at all three Model UN conferences that Rockhurst attended.
